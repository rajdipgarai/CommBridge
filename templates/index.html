    Gesture Cheat Sheet

    Hello: Open Palm

    Stop: Closed Fist

    Yes / No: Thumb Up / Down

    Help: Index Finger Up

    Water: V-Sign (2 Fingers)

    Food: 3 Fingers Up

    Thank You: 4 Fingers Up

    Call: Pinky Finger Only

    Toilet: 'T' Shape (Index Pointing Up)

    Washroom: Upside-down 'T' (Index Pointing Down)

    Light ON: 'L' Shape (Index & Thumb)

    OK: Pinch Thumb and Index

    Fan ON/OFF: Turn Palm Sideways

    Emergency: Shake Open Palm
